Visual Research

The two moodboards I generated are very different in the tone and mood they give off. I think they speak to slightly different audiences, although the look and feel of either moodboard I don't feel will drive a certain audience away from the site either. Below, I'll describe a bit of what my moodboards convey.

Moodboard 1

This first moodboard represents a more elegant, muted look and feel. Some of the images include a headshot, slightly symbolizing that I'm planning to have a spotlighted artist and designer on the landing page. The color and general direction are minimal, and the works are shown fairly simply. I like seeing just the color in the works, themselves, as well as having a small pop of color, too, so that the artist or designer and their works can have an even more prominent spotlight. The more muted pallette would serve as a nice vessel for any kind of artist or designer to take the light.
